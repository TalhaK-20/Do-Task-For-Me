FAQs

What is the website that solves any math problem?

There are no websites that can handle any math problem. However, there are several tools and platforms that can help you solve math issues. Here are some of them:

  • Wolfram Alpha. A powerful computational knowledge engine that solves loads of math problems, such as statistics, algebra, calculus, and so on. The website offers step-by-step solutions for some issues and deals with a wide assortment of topics. 
  • Symbolab. The math education platform provides students with step-by-step solutions to different math problems. You’re welcome to find help with calculus, algebra, trigonometry, and other issues. 
  • Mathway. A popular mathematics problem solver on the web. Mathway covers issues like statistics, calculus, algebra, etc. The platform enables you to enter your problem and get an instant explanation/solution.

What app identifies math problems?

One of the best apps to identify math problems is Photomath. This popular mobile app uses OCR (optical character recognition) technology to scan, process, and interpret math problems and equations. You can take a photo of a particular math question while Photomath will provide you with a detailed explanation and a range of solutions (including graphing capabilities for various topics in the niche).
